# Step 02 — Copywriting

## Input
Validated carousel structure from Step 01.

## Process

1. **Read brand config** — `resources/brand.md` for tone, language, word bans, accent color, accent font
2. **Invoke marketing-psychology** — Identify psychological levers for the topic (if installed, otherwise apply basic persuasion principles: anchoring, identity shift, loss aversion, curiosity gap, etc.)
3. **Write Slide 1 (Hook)** — This gets 90% of the effort:
   - Invoke copywriting skill (if installed, otherwise use hook formulas from `resources/copy-rules.md`)
   - Iterate with user until validated
4. **Write Slide 2 (Second Hook)** — Must work standalone or as continuation
5. **Write content slides** — One idea per slide, max 25 words
6. **Choose end slide type** — Save or Comment keyword (use CTA templates from brand.md)
7. **Annotate styling** — Mark accent words (accent font italic) and highlight words (accent color):
   ```
   **2026 is the year of the**       ← Primary Font Bold, white
   ~~Side Hustler~~                   ← strikethrough, white 35%
   *Builder*                          ← Accent Font italic, accent color
   ```
8. **Present full copy** in markdown for validation

## Rules
- Read `resources/copy-rules.md` before writing
- Use the tone and address form from brand.md
- Max accent words: 1-2 per slide (accent font italic)
- Max highlight words: 1 per slide (accent color)
- Audience pain points (from brand.md or context/) are the raw material

## Output
Complete copy for all slides with style annotations, validated by user.
